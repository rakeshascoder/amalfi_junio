SAFETY

A Global Team Watching Over Every Step

safety is a culture

Safety isn't just a keyword in an organization, safety must be a culture that runs deep in a company. From our Flight Operations to our Sales Team, everyone at Amalfi Jets goes through our internal safety training and utilizing our internal SMS system.

safety start on the ground

At Amalfi, safety starts on the ground. It begins with the training that all of our employees go through on our SMS System, Regulations, Risk Factors, and Risk Assessment.

We work with our Operator partners to identify trip risks while on the ground and work together on risk mitigation.

with you every step

Our Flight Operations Team is always paired 1:1 with a trip in progress. They remain watching over each trip from takeoff to landing and are there to assist in your every need.

Our Team can assist with last minute catering requests, transportation, or hospitality accommodations. We go where you go.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

In order to be able to fly our clients, our Safety Team verifies that the Operator, Aircraft, and Crew are within our trusted network. In addition to meeting all FAA regulations, we require that all Operators within our network hold additional third-party safety accreditation from the following companies: Argus, Wyvern, and IS-BAO. These three companies are the leading business aviation safety auditing firms and perform extensive due diligence on pilot training records, aircraft maintenance logs, and Operator safety procedures. If an Operator doesn’t have this certification, they don’t fly our clients.
