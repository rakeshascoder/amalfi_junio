Safety Runs Deep

Safety shouldn't be just a keyword, it's a culture that runs deep within an organization. At Amalfi, safety is at the forefront of everything we do. We recognize with various options in the market for a charter provider, safety varies per company. Within our aircraft network, we maintain strict standards for the Operator, Aircraft, and Flight Crew in order to be able to fly our clients.

Our internal safety management procedure, draws on our CEO's experience at Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University, where he currently maintains a role on the Industry Advisory Board for the College of Aviation.
